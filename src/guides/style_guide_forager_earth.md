# Design System: Forager Earth
**The Digital Greenhouse**

This design system is built to feel like a modern, high-end plant-based cookbook. It's warm, inviting, and grounded in nature.

## 1. Visual Language
*   **Aesthetic:** Warm, organic, tactile, and airy.
*   **Core Principles:** Generous whitespace, soft rounded corners, and subtle shadows for depth.

## 2. Color Palette
*   **Background:** Warm Cream (#FAF8F5) - Soft and inviting.
*   **Card Surfaces:** Pure White (#FFFFFF) - For clean content separation.
*   **Primary Accent:** Earthy Sage Green (#7D8C6F) - Used for primary actions, badges, and brand elements.
*   **Secondary Accent:** Warm Tan/Brown (#BFA88F) - For borders, secondary icons, and meta-text.
*   **Text:** Dark Charcoal (#333333) - High legibility without the harshness of pure black.

## 3. Typography
*   **Headings:** *Plus Jakarta Sans* (Bold/Extrabold) - Modern but with a slightly organic, friendly character.
*   **Body:** *Plus Jakarta Sans* (Medium/Regular) - Clean and contemporary.

## 4. UI Components
*   **Cards:** Rounded corners (16px), subtle 4px blur shadows.
*   **Buttons:** Fully rounded or 12px corners, solid sage green for primary actions.
*   **Navigation:** Clean top navbar with centered links and a subtle bottom border.
